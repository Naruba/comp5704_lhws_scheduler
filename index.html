Project Title: Latency-Hiding Work Stealing: Scheduling Interacting Parallel Computations with Work Stealing

Name: Vladimir Milicevic

E-Mail: Vladimir dot Milicevic at carleton dot ca

Project Outline: This work extends the standard model of parallel computations as Directed Acyclic Graphs (DAGs, or dags) by allowing edges to have non-unit weights that represent the latency incurred by an instruction. This work aims to show mathematically the behaviour of parallel threads that incur latency penalties, and develops a latency-hiding scheduling algorithm for such computation. The benefit of such a scheduler is the fine-grain ability to allow threads to suspend, and be overtaken by other threads on the hardware.
